Our Staff & Lecturers

Meet our dedicated team of educators and researchers who are committed to excellence in computing and informatics education.

Dr. John Smith

Head of Department

Dr. John Smith is the Head of the Department of Computing and Informatics at Bugema University. With over 15 years of experience in academia and industry, he specializes in software engineering and artificial intelligence. He holds a PhD in Computer Science from Makerere University and has published numerous papers in leading journals. Dr. Smith is passionate about bridging the gap between theory and practice in computing education.

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Senior Lecturer

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a Senior Lecturer specializing in artificial intelligence and machine learning. She earned her PhD from the University of Cambridge and has worked as a research scientist at leading tech companies. Her research focuses on natural language processing and computer vision applications in healthcare and education.
