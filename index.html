Overview

The Department of Computing and Informatics at Bugema University is part of the School of Science and Technology and focuses on teaching computer and information technology skills. The department prepares students to use technology to solve problems in different areas such as business, education, and communication.

It offers several programs including Bachelor of Information Technology, Bachelor of Software Engineering, Bachelor of Business Computing, and Diploma in Information Technology. Through practical training, research, and industry-based learning, the department helps students develop the knowledge and skills needed to work in the modern technology world.
